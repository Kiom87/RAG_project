# Healthllm — Health Insurance Product Guide

Healthllm is Insurellm's health insurance product for individuals and for employees of
small businesses (2–100 employees).

## Coverage
- Doctor visits, specialist visits, and telehealth.
- Hospitalisation and emergency care.
- Prescription drugs (tiered formulary).
- Preventive care at 100% (annual check-ups, vaccinations, screenings).

## Waiting Period
The standard **waiting period for the Healthllm plan is 90 days** from the policy start
date. During the waiting period, preventive and emergency care are covered, but elective
and non-emergency procedures are not yet eligible. The waiting period may be waived for
groups migrating from a prior qualifying plan with no gap in coverage.

## Eligibility
- Individuals aged 18–64 (dependents up to age 26 may be added).
- Small-business groups with at least 2 enrolled employees.
- A health questionnaire is required for individual plans; group plans are guaranteed issue.

## Plan Tiers
- **Bronze** — lower premium, higher deductible ($6,000 individual).
- **Silver** — balanced premium and deductible ($3,500 individual).
- **Gold** — higher premium, lower deductible ($1,500 individual).

## Pricing
Premiums depend on age, location, and selected tier. Small-business group rates start at
$280 per employee per month for the Silver tier.
