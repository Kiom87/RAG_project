# Insurellm — Company Overview

Insurellm is an insurance technology ("insurtech") company founded in 2015. We build
digital insurance products for individuals and small businesses, combining traditional
underwriting with modern software and automation.

## Mission
Make insurance simple, transparent, and fast — so customers understand exactly what
they are covered for and can file claims in minutes rather than weeks.

## What We Offer
Insurellm sells four core product lines:

- **Healthllm** — health insurance for individuals and employees of small businesses.
- **Cyberllm** — cyber liability insurance for small and medium businesses.
- **Autollm** — auto insurance for personal and commercial vehicles.
- **Bizllm** — bundled small-business insurance (property, liability, interruption).

## Company Facts
- Headquarters: Austin, Texas, USA.

- Customers served: over 50,000 policyholders.
