# Autollm — Auto Insurance Product Guide

Autollm provides auto insurance for personal and commercial vehicles.

## Coverage Types
- **Liability** — bodily injury and property damage to others.
- **Collision** — damage to your own vehicle from an accident.
- **Comprehensive** — theft, vandalism, weather, and animal collisions.
- **Roadside assistance** — towing, lockout, and jump-start (optional add-on).

## Eligibility
- Driver must hold a valid licence for at least 12 months.
- Commercial vehicles must be registered to a business with Insurellm.

## Discounts
- Safe-driver discount: up to 20% after 3 claim-free years.
- Multi-policy discount: 10% when bundled with Bizllm or Healthllm.
- Telematics ("Drive Smart") discount: up to 15% based on driving behaviour.

## Claims
Auto claims can be filed through the mobile app or the 24/7 claims hotline. Most
straightforward claims are assessed within 48 hours.
