# Cyberllm — Cyber Insurance Product Guide

Cyberllm is Insurellm's cyber liability insurance product, designed for small and
medium-sized businesses that handle customer data or rely on online operations.

## What It Covers
- Data breach response (notification, credit monitoring, forensics).
- Ransomware and cyber extortion.
- Business interruption caused by a cyber incident.
- Third-party liability (claims from customers whose data was exposed).
- Regulatory fines and penalties, where insurable by law.

## Coverage Limits
Cyberllm offers three coverage tiers:

- **Starter** — coverage limit of **$250,000** per policy year.
- **Growth** — coverage limit of **$1,000,000** per policy year.
- **Enterprise** — coverage limit of **$5,000,000** per policy year.

The default small-business recommendation is the **Growth** tier, with a **$1,000,000**
aggregate coverage limit and a $5,000 deductible per claim.

## Eligibility
- Registered businesses with fewer than 500 employees.
- Must have basic security controls in place: multi-factor authentication, regular
  backups, and an incident response contact.

## Small-Business Suitability
For small businesses, Insurellm most commonly bundles **Cyberllm (Growth tier)** with
**Bizllm** to cover both physical and digital risks in a single policy.
