# Claims Process

This document describes how customers and employees handle insurance claims at Insurellm.

## How to File a Claim
1. **Report** the incident through the Insurellm mobile app, the website, or the 24/7
   claims hotline (1-800-CLAIMS-0).
2. **Provide details** — policy number, date of incident, description, and any supporting
   documents or photos.
3. **Assessment** — a claims adjuster reviews the claim. Simple claims are assessed within
   48 hours; complex claims may take up to 10 business days.
4. **Decision and payout** — once approved, payouts are issued within 5 business days via
   direct deposit.

## Required Documents
- Policy number and policyholder ID.
- Proof of loss (photos, receipts, repair estimates, or medical bills).
- For auto claims: a police report when another party or injury is involved.

## Claim Status
Customers can track claim status in real time through the mobile app dashboard.

## Appeals
If a claim is denied, the policyholder may submit an appeal within 30 days, including any
new supporting evidence. Appeals are reviewed by a senior adjuster.
