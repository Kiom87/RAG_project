# Employee Handbook (Excerpts)

This handbook summarises key policies for Insurellm employees.

## Working Hours
Standard hours are Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm CT, with flexible start times between 7am and
10am. Remote and hybrid work is supported for most roles.

## Leave
- **Annual leave** — 20 paid days per year, accruing monthly.
- **Sick leave** — 10 paid days per year.
- **Parental leave** — 16 weeks paid for the primary caregiver.

## Employee Benefits
- Employees are automatically enrolled in **Healthllm (Gold tier)** after the standard
  **90-day waiting period**.
- Company contribution covers 80% of the employee health premium.
- 401(k) matching up to 5% of salary.

## Code of Conduct
Employees must protect customer data, follow security policies (including multi-factor
authentication), and report any suspected data breach to security@insurellm.example
immediately.

## Internal Knowledge
For product, policy, and regulatory questions, employees should consult the internal
knowledge base or the Insurellm AI Assistant rather than relying on memory.
