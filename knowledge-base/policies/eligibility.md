# Customer Eligibility Requirements

This document summarises eligibility rules across Insurellm products.

## General Requirements
- Applicant must be at least 18 years old.
- Applicant must reside or operate within a state where Insurellm is licensed.
- A valid form of identification is required.

## Product-Specific Eligibility
- **Healthllm** — individuals aged 18–64; small-business groups need at least 2 enrolled
  employees. Dependents may be added up to age 26.
- **Cyberllm** — businesses with fewer than 500 employees that have multi-factor
  authentication and regular backups in place.
- **Autollm** — drivers licensed for at least 12 months; commercial vehicles must be
  registered to a business.
- **Bizllm** — businesses with fewer than 100 employees.

## Ineligibility
Applications may be declined if there is a history of insurance fraud, if required
security controls are absent (for Cyberllm), or if the applicant operates in an excluded
high-risk category. Declined applicants may reapply after addressing the stated reason.
