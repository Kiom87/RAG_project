# Bizllm — Small Business Insurance Product Guide

Bizllm is Insurellm's bundled insurance product for small businesses. It combines several
common coverages into one policy to simplify protection for owners.

## What It Covers
- **General liability** — third-party injury and property damage claims.
- **Property** — damage to business premises, equipment, and inventory.
- **Business interruption** — lost income while the business cannot operate after a
  covered event.
- **Optional add-ons** — Cyberllm (cyber liability) and professional indemnity.

## Who It Is For
Bizllm is designed for businesses with fewer than 100 employees, including retail shops,
cafés, consultancies, and small workshops.

## Products Insurellm Offers for Small Businesses
Small businesses most often combine:
1. **Bizllm** — core property and liability protection.
2. **Cyberllm** — cyber liability (Growth tier recommended).
3. **Healthllm** — group health insurance for employees.

## Pricing
Bizllm starts at $95 per month for a basic general-liability-only plan, scaling with
revenue, property value, and selected add-ons.
